Cheese Maggi

The ultimate student comfort food. Just boil maggi noodles with taste-maker masala and dissolve some cheese in it.

Ingredients

  • maggi noodles
  • 2 taste-maker saches
  • 2 cheese slices
  • peas
  • carrots

Steps

  1. Place some peas and carrots in rice-cooker
  2. Take one set of maggi noodles in a bowl of water and add 2 saches of taste-maker maggi masala to it
  3. Bring on high heat and add 2 slices of cheese to the mixture. Stir vigorously to dissolve the cheese
  4. When peas and carrots are cooked add it to the noodle mixture
  5. When noodles are nice and soft take off the stove and serve
  6. Enjoy :)

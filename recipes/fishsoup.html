Clear Fish Noodle Soup

Simple fish clear soup which can comfort during a tough cold. Just boil fish and vegetables of your choice along with some aromatic spices to create a flavorful broth

Ingredients

  • basa fish fillets
  • sliced carrots
  • sliced bell peppers
  • star anise
  • fresh ground pepper
  • green chilly
  • butter

Steps

  1. Add fish fillets in a bowl with water
  2. Add sliced carrots and bell peppers
  3. Add 3 star anise, some fresh ground pepper and one sliced green chilly to the bowl
  4. Boil over high heat
  5. Once the fillets turn soft and opague in the middle remove the soup
  6. Add a slice of butter and stir till dissollved
  7. Enjoy and get well soon :)
